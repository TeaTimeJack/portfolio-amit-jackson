About Me

Amit Jackson

Fullstack Developer specializing in creating dynamic, scalable web applications using TypeScript, React, and Node.js. My core focus is on transforming complex business problems into elegant, efficient, and reliable code.

My Journey: For the past 5 years, I worked as a Crane Operator on construction sites. This role required me to operate heavy machinery, moving substantial cargo with absolute accuracy while maintaining the highest safety standards for everyone involved. The job demanded constant adaptation, as I moved between sites and worked with ever-changing teams, forging 2 critical skills: Uncompromising precision and the ability to Communicate clearly and lead diverse teams.

The Transition to Tech: Following my reserve duty after October 7th, I made the decisive choice to start a major career change. I realized I needed a role that would allow me to leverage my full potential for continuous self development.

For a Full Year, after completing my long shifts as a crane operator, I committed to self-study, learning technology independently until 12 or 1 AM. Recognizing this passion needed a professional outlet, I resigned from my previous job and enrolled in the intensive 4 month coding bootcamp at "Developer Institute," with the goal of becoming a professional programmer. I am immensely proud of this step and fully focused on applying my new technical expertise.

My Unconventional Background — including 5 years as a Crane Operator, my experience as an IDF Tank Commander, and Youth Coordinator—has forged a powerful and unique set of complementary skills that I bring to software development:

  • Precision and Detail: A deep, operational understanding of the need for absolute accuracy and zero-tolerance for errors in high-stakes operations and critical code.
  • Strategic Planning: The ability to look ahead, anticipate technical challenges, and map out a robust, reliable path forward for development projects.
  • Leadership and Coordination: Proven capacity to lead diverse teams, manage logistics, and ensure complex projects meet deadlines safely and successfully.
